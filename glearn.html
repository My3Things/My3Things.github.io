All About Gratitude

Gratitude is a great way to bring positive emotions and build resilience. This can look like appreciating what you have daily. For example: 

    "I am glad I have a roof over my head and my friend complimented 
    me on my outfit today!"

In every situation, there is an opportunity to appreciate what you have.
